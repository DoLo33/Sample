ABOUT MAX

Bio

Max Dolcelli

Comedy Genius

Max Dolcelli knows the art of comedy so if you are looking for a great, professional show, look no further!! He began his career at Pip’s in Brooklyn. Soonafter, he became a regular on the comedy scene in Manhattan. Max has performed at many comedy clubs in a variety of locations such as Montreal, Las Vegas, The Bahamas and Canada. His stage presence has been felt as a headliner for venues which include Comedy Zone, Punch Line, The Funny Bone, Caesar’s Palace, The Montreal Comedy Festival and Bananas just to name a few. In 1998, he was awarded an Appreciation Award at the Friars Club in New York City. Well-known entertainment contemporaries Max has opened for include Redd Foxx, Jerry Seinfeld, Robin Williams, Ray Romano, Tim Allen, Sam Kinison, Rosie O’Donnell, Little Richard and others. His television credits include Miami Vice, Night Heat (NBC), Comedy Central, NBC Young Comedian Special, Club 54 (Canadian television) and a host of morning talk shows. After 40 plus years of stand-up experience, Max continues to entertain crowds of comedy connoisseurs and make people laugh.
